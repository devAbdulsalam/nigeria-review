Modern Slavery Statement

At Reviews Nigeria, we are committed to combating modern slavery and human trafficking in all its forms. This Modern Slavery Statement outlines our efforts to prevent modern slavery within our operations and supply chains.

Our Commitment

We recognize that modern slavery is a global issue that requires a collective effort to eradicate. As such, Reviews Nigeria is committed to:
1. Zero Tolerance: We have a zero-tolerance policy towards modern slavery and human trafficking. We do not condone or support any form of exploitation or forced labor in our operations or supply chains.
2. Compliance: We comply with all relevant laws and regulations concerning modern slavery and human trafficking, including the Modern Slavery Act 2015.
3. Enforcement: We are committed to enforcing our zero-tolerance policy and ensuring that all employees and suppliers are trained and educated on modern slavery and human trafficking issues.
4. Awareness: We raise awareness among our employees and stakeholders about the risks of modern slavery and the importance of vigilance in identifying and reporting potential instances.

Our Operations

Within our own operations, we ensure that all employees are treated fairly and with dignity. We do not engage in any form of forced labor, child labor, or other forms of exploitation. Our employment practices are based on principles of equality, fairness, and respect for human rights.

Supply Chain

We recognize the importance of responsible sourcing and conduct due diligence to identify and mitigate risks of modern slavery within our supply chain. This includes:

1. Supplier Engagement:We work closely with our suppliers to ensure they adhere to ethical standards and comply with relevant laws and regulations.
2. Risk Assessment: We conduct risk assessments to identify high-risk areas within our supply chain and take appropriate actions to address any issues identified.
3. Monitoring and Auditing: We monitor and audit our suppliers regularly to ensure compliance with our standards and identify any areas for improvement.

Training and Education

We provide training and educational resources to our employees and suppliers to raise awareness about modern slavery and human trafficking. This includes training on recognizing the signs of exploitation, reporting procedures, and the importance of ethical sourcing.

Reporting and Accountability

We encourage a culture of transparency and accountability within our organization. Employees and stakeholders are encouraged to report any concerns or suspicions of modern slavery through our confidential reporting channels. All reports are investigated promptly, and appropriate actions are taken to address any violations identified.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to continuously improving our policies, procedures, and practices to prevent modern slavery and protect human rights. We regularly review and update our Modern Slavery Statement to ensure it remains relevant and effective.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding our GDPR compliance or the handling of personal data, please contact us.
